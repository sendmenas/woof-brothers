Inspiration

Main inspiration for this comics was Punisher game. Unfortunately there was no possibility to get any original comics books for this character, so the only way to continue the story was to make it by ourselves.

Various cartoons during our childhood were based on animal and non-human characters. This is the main reason why main characters in this comics are dogs, rats and etc.

World

The action take place in Bimern, Bimlortim. Bimern is a Capital of Bimlortim, a fiction country in a fiction world. There are other characters in this world as well, several other comic books were created after watching such movies like Terminator, Top Gun, Star Wars, various martial arts movies and etc. We had our own universe ☺.

Story

The story folows two brothers, Derek and Del, they are main characters of this comics. While living their lives they also try to bring justice and order in the society, so they are well known for various bad guys.

In this book brothers help to prevent bank robery by nutorious gansger Kraimking. You can find out how did they managed to do that by folowing their story in the home page.
